The sequel to LEGO Harry Potter Years 1-4, this LEGO game is a pretty
accurate adventure throughout part of the Harry Potter story.
Like all LEGO Travellers tales games, there are no word bubbles or chat recordings.
Instead, the story is told through mumbles and pantomimes.
The game avoids any violent or nasty scenes by using the
easy-to-take-apart qualities of LEGO minifigures.
Here are several differences when compared to LEGO Harry Potter Years 1-4:

*There is a small indicator that lets you know
when you're almost done with a Strength handle.
*Longer load times between scenes.
I can imagine it might be because of the next difference.
*The game starts off with Advanced Graphics selected,
rather than the Standard Graphics selected, which is excellent.

The biggest issue I have with this game is that it doesn't save my control preferences.

Otherwise, gameplay is intuitive and the graphics are enjoyable.
I recommend this for both Harry Potter and LEGO fans.
