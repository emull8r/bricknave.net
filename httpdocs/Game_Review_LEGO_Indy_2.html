From a Builder's perspective, this game is my favorite because of the level creator.
I suppose they didn't want to just make a game with new levels based on the
original movies and the fourth one, so they added a level
creator and a much larger character creator.
The level creator has everything from being able to program
switches via string-like elements to individual bricks that can be used to build things.

If you like all of the Indiana Jones films, or you are a LEGO fan
who craves building, I highly recommend this game.
