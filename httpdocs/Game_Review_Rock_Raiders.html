Rock Raiders is one of the most popular games I know of,as it has a fanbase dedicated to it.
It is a Real Time Strategy game, with a few of the concepts in LEGOLAND, but it is much more advanced.
Players must build bases using gathered resources,all the while guarding against creatures and landslides.
Some levels feature limited supplies of Oxygen,in which the player must soon build a oxygen-producingbuilding before their miners run out of air to breathe.
Players can also take control of vehicle and miners manually.
The one thing the game is missing is a Free Play environment,like what LEGOLAND has, because players are oftenrestricted to the level objectives.
